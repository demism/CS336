Dad By My Side

Confidence With Dad

This book exemplifies how I feel about my daughter and I. She finds confidence in me and I in her.

My daughter is my life. She's my reason.

Sometimes, work takes me away, sometimes I must travel. But no matter where I am I'll always make time for her. Blessed to be in dreams, and spending time with my daughter is my favorite activity.

No matter what we're doing we always have each other.

My daughter and I will rule the world one day. Together, we will occupy the dreams of others, and make our move. Together we shall perservere.
