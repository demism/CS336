My Story

Hello, my name is Freddy Kreuger. As halloween approaches I remember the great many dreams I embedded during the 80s and 90s. Today, I'm very much reformed, and although I still live in your dreams, I've come to the conclusion that there is greater benefits of being friendly and positive. Today, I enjoy fishing in your lakes, skiing in your mountains, and taking hot baths in the springs of Mt. Fuji. I ensure you, if you let me in your dreams, only enjoyment will come.

Secondly, I wanted to take a moment to apologize to all my victims who I have harmed in their dreams. I had a rough childhood, and I believed for the longest time that no one could be friendly. You have to remember I was burned alive. Luckily, myself, Jason, and a couple of my other friends from the 80s have come together and formed a book club, and rehabilitation group which truly enjoys devouring a good intellectual body of work. I hope to meet lots of you this Halloween.

Today, I live by my motto:

Sticks and stones may break my bones, but nothing will ever kill me.
